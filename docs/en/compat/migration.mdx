Years of work, dozens of addons, an economy, players who know your server by heart. All of it deserves better than a 2004 engine. **reborn-migrate** carries your entire server to s&box, in one command, in minutes.

```bash
python3 tools/reborn-migrate/reborn_migrate.py "C:/servers/garrysmod"
```

The tool speaks English, français and русский. It inventories, converts, resolves, and hands you a verdict report: you know exactly where you stand before you even start your server.

## What happens during those few minutes

<Steps>
	<Step title="Full inventory">
		Addons (folders and `.gma` archives), custom gamemodes, loose scripts, Workshop
		collections, database, configuration, startup map. Nothing is missed and nothing
		leaves your disk.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Smart conversion">
		Every addon becomes a DarkRP Reborn addon. Scripted entities and weapons follow.
		Addons that share functions with each other are detected and grouped
		automatically so they keep working together, just like on GMod.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Your server stays YOURS">
		Custom HUD, homemade F4 menu, scoreboard, levels, gangs: if your server has its
		own version, that version stays active. Duplicated stock systems are switched
		off, and your server loads faster.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Report + deployment">
		A per-addon verdict report, a ready-to-run deploy script, then `addon_reload`
		on the server. Your players get THEIR server back.
	</Step>
</Steps>

## What migrates

| Your GMod server | On s&box | |
|---|---|---|
| Lua addons (folders and `.gma`) | DarkRP Reborn addons | ✅ automatic |
| Jobs, economy, commands, hooks | Work as-is | ✅ automatic |
| Scripted entities (SENT) and weapons (SWEP) | Playable | ✅ automatic |
| Custom HUD and 2D interfaces | Render natively | ✅ automatic |
| `sv.db` database (money, doors, jobs) | Imported | ✅ automatic |
| Full `darkrpmodification` | Replicated content | ✅ automatic |
| Models, textures, materials | Converted in the same command, skeletons and animations included | ✅ automatic |
| Interdependent addons | Grouped and ordered automatically | ✅ automatic |
| Very custom 3D effects | Listed precisely in the report | 🎨 guided |

## Tested on a real server, not a demo

We ran a complete server through the tool: **71 public addons plus an entire gamemode** (450+ Lua files). Outcome: 72 converted units, the overwhelming majority loads and runs immediately, groupings happen on their own, and the report pinpoints the little that deserves a touch-up. Zero conversion errors.

## After the migration

<Steps>
	<Step title="Run the deployment">
		`deploy.sh` is generated pre-filled: it ships addons, configuration and data to
		your server.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Reload">
		Server console: `addon_reload` then `addon_list`. Every addon must be `Loaded`.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Import content">
		`import_content.sh` fetches and converts your Workshop models
		([details](/en/compat/import-gmod)).
	</Step>
	<Step title="Polish">
		Every addon that deserves a touch-up has its own `PORTING.md` with the exact
		lines. You always know what to do next.
	</Step>
</Steps>
