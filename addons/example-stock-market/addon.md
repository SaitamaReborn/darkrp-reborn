# Stock market

- **Ident**: `example-stock-market`
- **Author**: DarkRP Reborn
- **Version**: 1.0
- **Description**: A small stock exchange showing the addon UI framework:
  `/bourse` opens a themed window (game design by default, overridable), prices
  drift, Buy/Sell orders round-trip to the server, and a HUD card tracks the
  portfolio. State persists across restarts.

Reference addon for **contract v3**: menus (`RebornMenu`, tables, inputs,
buttons), HUD cards, keyed persistence, timers.
